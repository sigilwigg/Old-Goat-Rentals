Who Are We?

We are a family-run, local car rental company that specializes in providing used vehicles as an eco-friendly and cost-effective option for your vacation needs!

How Does It Work?

Step 1: Book The Car

Our "Book Now" button will take you to our Outdoorsy.com profilewhere you can select your desired car and any accessories you need for your stay on Maui.

Step 2: Pick It Up When You Land

We will prepare everything for you! We fill the tank and leave the car for you to pick up at the Airport (convenient!).

Step 3: Enjoy Maui

Explore the island! When you take the car to the airport to fly home, just park the car in the airport parking lot with a full tank and send us a picture of the car's location. That's it! Done!
